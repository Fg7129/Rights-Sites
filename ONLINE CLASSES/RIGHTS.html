HUMAN RIGHTS.

Human rights are freedoms ,and privelleges that people need to live a normal life.Human rights are protected in the Kenyan Constitution.The main right is the right to life.

BASIC HUMAN RIGHTS.


  1. Right to life-(main)
  2. Right to food.
  3. Right to shelter
  4. Right to cloth.
  5. Right to security.
  6. Right to education.
  7. Right to expression.
  8. Right to movement.
  9. Right to worship.
  10. Right to vote.
  11. Right to be loved.

THE IMPORTANCE OF HUMAN RIGHTS.


  1. Make people to live in peace.
  2. Make people feel secure.
  3. Promote democracy.
  4. Makes people to be equal.
  5. Protects children from abuse.

CLASSIFICATION OF HUMAN RIGHTS.

Human rights can be classified into three categories.These are political,socail and economic rights.

POLITICAL RIGHTS INCLUDE:

  1. Right to life.
  2. Freedom to worship.
  3. Right to fair trial.
  4. Right to equality.
  5. Right to vote.
  6. Freedom of movement.
  7. Freedom of speech.
  8. Freedom of association.

SOCIAL RIGHTS INCLUDE:

  1. Right to have or belong to a famiy.
  2. Right to a name and culture.
  3. Right to privacy.
  4. Right to education.
  5. Right to recreaton and entertainment.
  6. Right to health care.
  7. Right to shelter.
  8. Right to food.
  9. Right to clean water.

ECONOMIC RIGHTS INCLUDE:

  1. Right to own property.
  2. Right to trade.
  3. Right to work.
  4. Right to fair wages and equal pay.
  5. Right to belong to a workers union.

  3. Child prostitution.
  4. Child neglect.

  6. Wrongful arrest and torture.
  7. Discrimination and favourtism.
  8. Denying people food.

  10. Exposing children to drugs and substance use.
